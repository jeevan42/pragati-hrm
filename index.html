Features Of Pragati HRM

1

Attendance Tracking

Time and Attendance Management

Helps monitor employee attendance, track working hours, and manage time-off records.
Pragati HRM goes beyond simple time tracking. It offers insightful reports and analytics that provide valuable data on employee attendance patterns, time allocation, and productivity trends.

2

Leave management

Accurate Leave Balance Calculation

Pragati HRM simplifies leave management, ensuring a seamless process for employees and HR personnel. With our user-friendly interface, employees can easily submit leave requests, while approvers can efficiently review and approve them. This system keeps track of leave balances and provides timely notifications, making leave management a breeze.

3

Notifications & Reminders

Real-Time Notifications & Reminders

When modifications occur in employee information, such as contact details or banking information, our system promptly sends you notifications, ensuring you stay up-to-date and maintain accurate records. For project and task management, you'll receive real-time alerts whenever new projects or tasks are assigned to you, enabling you to stay on track and meet your commitments.

4

Meeting Scheduling

Google Calendar Integration

Allows users to schedule meetings by selecting the date, time, and duration, and sends automated invitations to participants.
With our Google Calendar API integration, you can now sync your HR-related events, meetings, and important dates directly with your Google Calendar. This means you no longer have to manually transfer information between systems or worry about missing any crucial appointments.

5

Flag Raised System

Work Hour Flag Raised System

This system serves as a proactive mechanism to address potential issues related to overtime, workload management, and employee well-being. It allows managers and HR personnel to promptly review and address instances where employees may be consistently working beyond their scheduled hours.

6

Project and Task Management

Project Management and
Task System

Pragati HRM revolutionizes project management and task system, empowering your organization to achieve new levels of efficiency and productivity. With our advanced project and task feature, you can effortlessly plan, track, and manage all your projects from a single centralized platform.

7

Work Calendar

Comprehensive Work Calendar

Pragati HRM includes a powerful and comprehensive Work Calendar feature that serves as a centralized hub for managing employee attendance, holidays, leaves, flag raised incidents, and meetings. With this feature, you can efficiently track and organize all aspects of your workforce's schedule and activities in one place.
